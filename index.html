How Does This Work?

Step 1: Capture Your Image

  • Take a Photo: Use your device's camera to capture a full-body image. Ensure you are in a well-lit area for the best results.
  • Upload Image: Alternatively, you can upload an existing image from your device.

Step 2: Body Analysis

  • Processing: Our advanced computer vision algorithms analyze your body shape and measurements from the uploaded image.
  • Shape Detection: We identify key features such as shoulders, waist, and hips to determine your body shape.

Step 3: Style Recommendations

  • Personalized Suggestions: Based on your body shape, we curate a selection of fashion dresses that complement your figure.
  • Browse Options: Explore the recommended styles, including different colors, patterns, and sizes.

Step 4: Make Your Choice

  • Select Your Favorite: Click on any dress to view more details, including fabric, price, and customer reviews.
  • Add to Cart: If you find a dress you love, add it to your cart for easy checkout!

Step 5: Enjoy Your New Look

  • Purchase and Delivery: Complete your purchase, and your selected dresses will be shipped directly to you.
  • Fashion Tips: Don't forget to check our styling tips for how to wear your new dress!

UNDERSTAND YOUR BODY SHAPE

Hourglass

The hourglass body shape is characterized by a well-defined waist with balanced bust and hip measurements.

  • Shoulders align with hips
  • Defined waistline
  • Curved hip line
  • Proportionate upper and lower body

Pear

The pear body shape features hips that are wider than the bust, with a defined waist.

  • Narrow shoulders and bust
  • Defined waistline
  • Fuller hips and thighs
  • Rounded bottom

Inverted Triangle

The inverted triangle body shape has a bust that is wider than the hips, with a less defined waist.

  • Broad shoulders
  • Larger bust
  • Narrow hips
  • Slender legs

Rectangle

The rectangle body shape has balanced bust and hips with a less defined waist, creating a straight silhouette.

  • Shoulders align with hips
  • Minimal waist definition
  • Even distribution of weight
  • Athletic build

Apple

The apple body shape features a fuller bust and waist with narrower hips, often with slim legs.

  • Broader shoulders
  • Fuller bust and waist
  • Narrow hips
  • Slender legs
